A complete recipe to make MO:MO

Many of you have heard of the word dumpling, which comes off as a bun with stuffing inside of it. Its origin comes from China, and there is a high possibility that many have tried it once. This time we will go through the process of creating Nepalese dumplings called MO:MO as it is originated in Nepal, which is between China and India. So it has the style of China and mix of spices like India created by the people of Nepal.

Ingredients of Chicken/Buffalo MO:MO

Total Cook time 40/50 min

  • 500 gram chicken/buffalo, minced
  • 200 grams of cabbage
  • 100 grams carrot
  • 4 cloves of garlic/roasted
  • garam masala/spices according to need
  • salt and wonton coverings
  • cooking oil
  • one steamer

Process and Time

It takes a total of 40 minutes to complete all the processes.
10 minutes of prep time.
30 minutes of cook time.
It can be divided between 4 people.

Prep
Grind all the ingredients until you get a granular paste.
On a chopping board lay down wonton coverings and put a small teaspoon of theshilly oil .
