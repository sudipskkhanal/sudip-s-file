Ingredients of Chicken/Buffalo MO:MO

Total Cook time 40/50 min

  • Ingredients;
    • 500 gram chicken/buffalo, minced
    • 200 grams of cabbage
    • 100 grams carrot
    • 4 cloves of garlic/rosted
    • garam masala/ spices according to need
    • salt and a wonton coverings
    • cooking oil
    • one steamer

    • Process and time

    it takes total of 40 minutes to complete all the process.
    10 minute of Prep time.
    30 minute of cook time.
    It can be divided in between 4 People.

    Prep
    grind all the ingredients until you get a granular paste.
    on a chopping board law down wonton covering and put small teaspoon of the above paste on it individually.
    close each cobering with any design as you like.
    cook time
    spray cooking oil in the surface of the steamer and place down the MO:MO.
    Allow it to steam for 5-7 minute. serve it with hotsauce or chilli oil
